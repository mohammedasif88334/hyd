1. Hyderabadi Biryani is a famous rice dish from Hyderabad known for its rich spices and aroma.
  2. It is usually made with basmati rice, marinated meat (mostly chicken or mutton), and saffron.
  3. The dish is cooked using the "dum" method, where rice and meat are steamed together in a sealed pot.
  4. It is often served with raita (yogurt sauce), salad, and spicy gravy called mirchi ka salan.

  1. Irani Chai is a special tea popular in Hyderabad, brought by Persian (Irani) immigrants.
  2. It is made with strong brewed tea, lots of milk, and sugar, giving it a rich and creamy taste.
  3. The tea is slowly simmered to get a thick, smooth texture.
  4. It is usually enjoyed with Osmania biscuits, tie biscuits, or bun maska.
  5. Irani cafes in Hyderabad, especially around Charminar, are famous spots to enjoy this chai.
